Static = Default positioning

Does not move

Absolute positioning

I am positioned to the viewport or window. Used for Logos as you can tell it to stay where you want it. It disapears as you scroll

Fixed positioning

I am fixed even if you scroll, I am positioned relative to the accesskey. Used for Nav or back to top button as the item doesnt leave the position as you scroll. Can be used for pop up subscribe or log in boxes

Relative positioning

I am positioned relative to where I am usually placed.
